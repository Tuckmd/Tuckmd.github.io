This talk describes my work on the simultaneous quantification of the anti-tuberculosis drugs rifampicin, ethambutol, and clofazimine using MALDI mass spectrometry imaging. As the preceding presentation focused on nanoscale machines, I began by noting that the spatial scales I study are about an order of magnitude larger.
